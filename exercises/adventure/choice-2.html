The Path


You take the gold before the king. Cautious of you at first, upon upon inspection the king recognizes a family airloom among the contents of the chest. A wedding gift for a relative in another kingdom. The chest must have been lost on it's way to it's destination. The king is overjoyed he offers to grant you one request, whatever you desire he will give you. You think of you're life, how much sweeter it would be if you had your own castle a lord, a noble with servants, gold, and the tastiest delicacies straight from the kings kitchen. But what of you're village? Little food to go around, a dying economy, and disease runs rampant. You have the power to change their lives... What will it be?
