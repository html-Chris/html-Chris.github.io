1. ICE CUBE SANDWHICH

Creator: For real?   Prep Time: 60 seconds... unless you didn't fill the ice tray.   Makes: 1

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 slices of loafed bread
  • A handful of ice cubes

DIRECTIONS

  1. Take out 2 slices of loafed bread.
  2. Place handful of ice cubes on 1 slice of bread.
  3. Press slices together to make a sandwhich and enjoy.

2. HOT DAWG SPAGHET

Creator: Pintrest?   Prep Time: 8-12 minutes   Makes: 1 cup

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup of Spaghetti noodles
  • 1-2 hot dogs
  • A dash of salt

DIRECTIONS

  1. Cut 1-2 uncooked hot dogs into 1 inch slices.
  2. Take 8-10 uncooked spaghetti noodles and skewer the hot dog slices (repeat until all slices are skewered and all pasta is used.)
  3. Add a dash of salt to water and boil hot dog spaghetti on high for 7-10 minutes or until soft and dogs are fully cooked.

3. FISH 'N' CHIPS

Creator: Definitely someones child   Prep Time: 60 seconds or less   Makes: A bowl

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 bag of gold fish (6.6 oz, we ain't Willy Wonka up in this piece)
  • 1 bag of Ruffles Original chips (8.6oz)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Take 1 bowl and dump the entire bag of Ruffles and Goldfish into it.
  2. Enjoy.
